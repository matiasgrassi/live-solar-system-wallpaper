Privacy Policy

Solar System Wallpaper values your privacy. This policy describes how data is handled within the application.

1. Data Collection & Usage

  • Anonymous Technical Data: To ensure app stability and fix technical issues, the app uses Firebase Crashlytics and Firebase Analytics (provided by Google). This sends anonymous information when the app crashes or fails to update the wallpaper. This data includes device model, OS version, and technical error logs. It does not include personal information, names, photos, or files.
  • Wallpaper Updates: The app modifies the wallpaper and/or lock screen locally on the device based on your manual settings.
  • Astronomical Data: The app accesses the device’s UTC time locally to calculate and update astronomical positions. This calculation is performed entirely on your device.

2. Data Sharing

  • No personal or sensitive user data is collected, stored, or shared by the developer.
  • Anonymous technical diagnostic data is shared only with Google (Firebase) for the sole purpose of monitoring app performance and improving stability.

3. Network Connectivity

  • The app requires an internet connection solely for the purpose of transmitting the anonymous technical reports mentioned above. No user-generated content or personal files are ever transmitted.

4. Battery Optimization

  • The app may request permission to be excluded from battery optimizations. This is used strictly to ensure that the wallpaper updates can occur periodically in the background as intended by the user.

This policy may be updated if app functionality changes. Last updated: June 2024
